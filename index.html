About me

I am bachelor student of Sharif University of Technology, and I'm studying Major of Computer engineering and Minor of Mathematics, I had four internships with Max Planck Institute under supervision of Dr. Singla.
I am interested in Statistics, Machine learning, Deep learning, and optimization.
As a hobby I play Iranian traditional instrument Santoor. I love hiking, swimming, biking, badminton, table tennis, and table football. Recently started bouldering, also I occasionally draw.
For more information please check my CV.
